About PadolinaKonektado

Building bridges between communities and technology for a better tomorrow.

Our Mission

To create a seamless digital platform that empowers barangay communities through efficient communication, transparent governance, and active citizen participation. We aim to bridge the gap between residents and local officials, making community management more accessible and effective.

Our Vision

To be the leading community engagement platform that transforms how Filipino barangays operate, fostering stronger relationships, promoting transparency, and creating smarter, more connected communities across the nation.

What We Offer

Comprehensive features designed for modern communities

Community Communication

Real-time messaging system that allows residents to communicate directly with barangay officials. Share concerns, ask questions, and stay updated with official announcements.

Concern Management

Efficient system for reporting and tracking community concerns. Residents can submit issues with photos and locations, while officials can update status and provide solutions.

Event Coordination

Comprehensive event management system for community activities. Create events, send invitations, track attendance, and share photos and updates with the community.

Document Management

Secure storage and easy access to important barangay documents, permits, and announcements. Residents can download necessary forms and documents anytime.
